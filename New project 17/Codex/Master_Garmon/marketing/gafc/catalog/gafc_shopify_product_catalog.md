## Operating Model

GAFC is a waitlist-first apparel and merch brand for now. Use POD-style mockups and Shopify demand capture to validate interest before ordering physical inventory. Local drops are planned after the waiting list shows which products people want.

Operating implications:

- Do not imply products are sitting in local inventory unless inventory is actually held.
- Set customer expectations around production time, shipping time, sizing, and returns.
- Use demand testing before expanding designs, variants, or paid campaigns.
- Favor collections, mockups, launch lists, bundles, and limited-time campaigns before ordering.
- Track product interest before committing to bulk ordering.
- After enough waiting-list demand exists, order selected items and run a local drop.
