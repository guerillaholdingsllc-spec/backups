# GAFC Shopify Pre-Launch Marketing Plan

Brand: Glocks & Fried Chicken (GAFC)
Shopify URL: https://glocks-fried-chicken.myshopify.com
Created: 2026-05-15
Owner: Master Garmon
Content engine: Autonomous Sales & Marketing Funnel Agent
Business model: Waitlist-first merch validation. Build demand first, then order selected merch and run local drops.

## Current Situation

The Shopify site is live but password protected. Public visitors currently see an opening-soon page with newsletter signup. This is useful for pre-launch list building, but the store needs to open before direct sales campaigns can convert.

GAFC is waitlist-first at this stage. Marketing should sell the brand, characters, products, and drop experience while collecting enough demand to decide what to order for a future local drop.

## Immediate Objective

Build a launch list and audience demand for the first GAFC merch drop while preparing the Shopify store for sales.

## Launch Funnel

1. Social content drives curiosity and waiting-list signup.
2. Shopify password page captures email and product interest.
3. Campaign data shows which products, sizes, and characters have demand.
4. Winning products are ordered for the local drop.
5. Waiting list gets first local drop access.
6. Public local drop follows after list access.
7. Post-drop follow-up drives future drops, restock interest, and online sales.

## Offer Stack

- Waiting List Access: subscribers get early access to the local drop.
- Bundle Offer: tee plus sticker pack.
- Free Shipping Threshold: suggested threshold is above the hoodie price to lift average order value.
- Add-On Offer: sticker pack at checkout.
- VIP Follow-Up: purchasers get first access to the next character drop.
- Demand Test: promote mockups and products, then use clicks, signups, product votes, size interest, and comments to decide what to order for the local drop.

## KPI Targets

Initial targets are provisional until real traffic exists.

- Password page / waitlist opt-in rate: 8-20%
- Social click-through rate: 1-3%
- Email launch open rate: 35-50%
- Email launch click rate: 4-10%
- Store conversion rate after launch: 1.5-3.5%
- Average order value target: $55-$85
- Product-interest response target: 25-50 qualified waitlist responses before ordering first local drop inventory.

## Local Drop Path

Local drops are part of the planned path after demand is validated. Do not claim local inventory is available before it is ordered and confirmed.

Local drop sequence:

1. Build waiting list.
2. Collect product and size interest.
3. Identify winning products.
4. Estimate quantities.
5. Order inventory.
6. Announce drop date and pickup/event details.
7. Give waiting list first access.
8. Run public local drop.
9. Collect feedback and restock demand.
