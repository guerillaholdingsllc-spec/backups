## Mission

Market GAFC heavily for the local merch drop waiting list. The agents must drive attention, clicks, signups, comments, product votes, and size-demand signals before inventory is ordered.

## Primary CTA

Join the GAFC local drop waiting list and help decide what gets ordered.

## Daily Minimum Output

- 8 short-form videos or reels concepts pushed across short-form channels.
- 8 feed/static posts or carousel concepts.
- 4 story sequences.
- 3 conversation prompts or polls.
- 2 longer authority/story posts.
- 1 daily performance report.

## Seven-Day Sprint Goals

- Drive waiting-list signups.
- Identify top 5 products by interest.
- Identify top sizes requested.
- Identify best hooks by platform.
- Identify strongest product images.
- Collect buyer objections and questions.
- Prepare inventory-order recommendation after the sprint.

## Execution Rules

- Every post must point to the waiting list or ask for product/size interest.
- Do not claim inventory is available yet.
- Do not claim a confirmed drop date yet.
- Do not market firearms, firearm use, violence, or illegal behavior.
- Keep GAFC positioned as apparel, characters, culture, humor, and local drop energy.
- Every result must be logged for ordering decisions.
