# GAFC Creator + Venue Outreach Bank

Created: 2026-05-18
Use: Agent 19 creator outreach and Agent 20 venue outreach

## Creator Target Lists

Tier A: Sacramento culture creators
- Streetwear fit pages.
- Sneaker reviewers.
- Barbers.
- DJs.
- Nightlife photographers.
- Food reviewers.
- Skaters.
- Car meet creators.
- Local meme pages.

Tier B: Regional expansion
- Roseville, Elk Grove, Folsom, Rocklin, Citrus Heights, North Highlands, Meadowview, Del Paso Heights, Broderick.

Tier C: Conversion partners
- Creators with an email list, Discord, SMS list, or local event pull.

## Creator Qualification

Green light:
- 2,000-50,000 followers.
- Comments look real.
- Has posted local businesses or merch before.
- Has a clear visual style.
- Would plausibly wear or talk about GAFC.

Skip:
- Engagement looks fake.
- Page is mostly reposted memes with no local pull.
- Content centers violence, weapons, or illegal activity.
- Creator needs a brand fit that would push GAFC into biker/MC territory.

## Creator DM 1

`Yo [NAME], GAFC is building a Sacramento character streetwear drop: Gloxsie, Bobo, Cardey, Keezey, SB, and Cuppy. Limited pieces, address-by-SMS drops, raffle-only SKUs you cannot buy. Your page fits the local culture. Want early access and a creator code for the first drop?`

## Creator DM 2

`Not a mass blast. We are picking a small local creator group before the public drop. You would get preview assets, early access, and a trackable creator link. Want me to send the preview?`

## Creator Brief

Post angle:
- "Would you wear this?"
- "Which character should headline the first drop?"
- "Sacramento, are you pulling up if the address drops by SMS?"

Required CTA:
- Join the list.
- Comment product or character.
- Tag neighborhood.

Do not say:
- In stock now.
- Guaranteed resale.
- Anything promoting firearms or violence.

## Venue Target Lists

Best fit:
- Liquor stores.
- Smoke shops.
- Sneaker shops.
- Barbershops.
- Independent restaurants.
- Local event spaces.
- Car wash or detail shops with visible lots.
- Record stores or DJ-friendly spaces.

## Venue Qualification

Green light:
- Owner/manager can approve quickly.
- Safe line area.
- Exterior photo/video potential.
- Open to co-branded promo.
- Will respect address-by-SMS rule.
- Can handle 20-30 unit post-event consignment.

## Venue DM 1

`Peace [NAME], GAFC is booking limited local streetwear drops at independent Sacramento spots. We bring RSVP/SMS traffic, co-branded promo, a venue-exclusive SKU, day-of revenue share, and post-event consignment. Your spot came up as a fit. Who handles collabs or events?`

## Venue DM 2

`Quick version: GAFC handles the drop content, SMS list, scarcity, and merch. Venue gets traffic, promo, revenue share, exclusive SKU, and first right of refusal for the next territory drop. Can we walk the space this week?`

## Venue Offer Summary

- 15-20% revenue share on day-of merch sales.
- Co-brand exposure in countdown and recap content.
- Venue-exclusive SKU.
- First right of refusal for the next territory drop.
- 20-30 post-event consignment units at 30% venue margin.

## Call Script

`GAFC is a Sacramento character streetwear brand. We run limited-quantity drops where the address only goes to the SMS list 24 hours before. That keeps the crowd controlled and makes the event feel exclusive. We are looking for independent venues that want traffic, content, and a rev share without having to run the merch operation themselves.`

## Close

`If the space works, we can hold [DATE] and start teasing the venue without releasing the address. Your public reveal happens with the 24-hour SMS release and recap content after the event.`
