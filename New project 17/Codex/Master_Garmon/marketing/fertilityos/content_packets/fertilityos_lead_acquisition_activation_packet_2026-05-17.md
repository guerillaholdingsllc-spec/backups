## Compliance Guardrails

- Required footer or caption phrase: "Educational only. Not medical advice, diagnosis, treatment guidance, insurance advice, or financing approval."
- Use "questions to ask," "planning," "comparison," "organize," "prepare," and "learn" as safe verbs.
- Avoid "improve fertility," "boost sperm," "best clinic," "guaranteed approval," "covered by insurance," "increase pregnancy odds," and "recommended treatment."
- Do not request sensitive medical details in public comments. Move users to the live form when appropriate.
- For buyer-facing language, refer to "consented inquiries" and "structured intent," not patient acquisition promises.
