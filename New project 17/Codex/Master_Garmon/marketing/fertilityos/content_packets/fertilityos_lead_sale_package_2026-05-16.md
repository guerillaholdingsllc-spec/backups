## Current Lead Handling

FertilityOS lead flow is now:

1. User submits inquiry on FertilityFund, MaleGenesis, or IVFCompare.
2. Lead is stored in shared Supabase backend.
3. User receives a Brevo auto-response confirming someone will follow up within 24-48 hours.
4. Lead remains available for handoff to a buyer, partner, clinic, lender, benefits platform, or broker.

Do not promise instant approval, medical advice, insurance coverage, clinical outcome, or treatment recommendation.

## Handoff Guardrails

- Do not sell or transfer leads to partners who cannot follow up responsibly.
- Require a clear buyer category and follow-up SLA.
- Keep a record of buyer, lead batch, price, transfer date, and allowed use.
- Do not include sensitive medical claims or diagnosis fields.
- Use anonymized samples until a buyer agreement is in place.
